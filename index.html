F.A.Q

  • What is the International STEM & Robotic Championship (ISRC)?

    The International STEM & Robotic Championship (ISRC) is a global competition that brings together students and enthusiasts from around the world to showcase their skills and knowledge in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) through various robotic challenges and innovative activities.

  • Who can participate in the ISRC?

    The ISRC is open to students and teams from schools, colleges, and universities, as well as individual participants who are passionate about robotics and STEM fields. There are different categories and levels of competition to accommodate participants of varying ages and skill levels.

  • How can I register for the ISRC?

    You can register for the ISRC by visiting our official website and filling out the registration form available on the "Register" page. Detailed instructions and eligibility criteria are provided to help you complete the registration process.

  • What are the different categories and challenges in the ISRC?

    The ISRC features a variety of categories and challenges, including robotic programming, innovation activities, drone competitions, and more. Each category is designed to test specific skills and knowledge areas, providing a comprehensive platform for participants to demonstrate their abilities.

  • Where and when will the ISRC events take place?

    The ISRC events are held in multiple stages, starting with regional and state-level championships, followed by national-level events and culminating in the international championship. The first event of the year will be hosted in Mumbai, with subsequent state-level winners advancing to the national championship at AIKTC in Panvel, and the international championship being held in Dubai.

  • What resources are available to help participants prepare for the ISRC?

    The ISRC provides a range of resources to help participants prepare for the competition, including online tutorials, workshops, webinars, and access to our community forums where participants can collaborate and share ideas. Additionally, we offer past competition materials and guidelines to help teams understand the requirements and standards of the championship.
